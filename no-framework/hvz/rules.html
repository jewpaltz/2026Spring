🎮 Basic Gameplay

+

Humans vs Zombies is a game of tag played across campus. All players start as humans except for one "Original Zombie" (OZ). Zombies try to tag humans to convert them. Humans defend themselves with Nerf blasters and sock balls. The game runs 24/7 for one week. Safe zones include: inside buildings, dining halls during meals, classrooms during class, and residence hall rooms. The outdoor areas of campus are always active!

🎯 Tagging Rules

+

For Zombies: A tag is a firm touch on any part of the human's body or clothes. Once tagged, the human must give you their ID code. Report the tag on the website within 1 hour. You cannot tag while stunned.

For Humans: When tagged, you become a zombie immediately. Move your bandana to your head. Wait 15 minutes before you can start hunting. This is your "transformation period."

🔫 Approved Equipment

+

Approved Blasters: Any Nerf-brand or equivalent foam dart blaster. No modifications that increase power. Must pass moderator inspection.

Sock Balls: Rolled-up socks. Must be soft and throwable. No weights or hard objects inside.

NOT Allowed: Airsoft, paintball, water guns, melee weapons, shields, or any modified equipment. When in doubt, ask a moderator!

⚡ Stunning Zombies

+

Hit a zombie with a dart or sock to stun them. Stunned zombies must sit down or kneel and count to 15 minutes (or set a timer). They cannot move, tag, or interfere with gameplay while stunned. Zombies must be honest about being stunned! Disputes should be reported to moderators. Multiple hits don't extend stun time.

🏠 Safe Zones

+

Always Safe:
• Inside any academic building
• Residence hall rooms (not hallways)
• Dining halls during meal hours
• Library during finals week
• Any classroom during scheduled class
• Campus shuttles and buses

Never Safe:
• Parking lots
• The quad and all outdoor areas
• Building lobbies and hallways
• Athletic fields (except during official games)

🎭 The Original Zombie (OZ)

+

The OZ starts the outbreak. Their identity is SECRET for the first 48 hours. They wear their bandana on their arm like a human but are actually a zombie. After 48 hours or once they make 3 tags (whichever comes first), they must reveal themselves and wear the bandana on their head. The OZ is immune to starvation for the first 3 days.

💀 Zombie Starvation

+

Zombies must feed (successfully tag a human) at least once every 48 hours or they "starve" and are eliminated from the game. Starved zombies can return as "spectator zombies" who can participate in missions but cannot tag humans. This mechanic prevents zombies from hiding and waiting until the end of the game.

🎯 Missions

+

Special missions run throughout the game. These are announced via the website and Discord. Mission types include:

• Escort: Protect a VIP from point A to B
• Capture: Hold a location for a set time
• Retrieval: Find and return objects to base
• Siege: All-out battle at a specific location

Completing missions earns points and can unlock special abilities!

⚠️ Code of Conduct

+

• No playing in prohibited areas (labs, offices, etc.)
• No physical force beyond a light tag
• No entering someone's personal vehicle or residence
• No interfering with non-players
• No lying about tags or stuns (honor system!)
• Respect moderator decisions
• Most importantly: HAVE FUN and be a good sport!

Violations may result in removal from the game.

❓ Quick FAQ

Common questions answered

Can I play if I have class?

Absolutely! Classrooms are safe zones during class. Just plan your routes carefully between buildings!

What if I can't afford a blaster?

Sock balls are free! We also have a loaner program. No one is excluded for financial reasons.

Can zombies run?

Yes! Zombies can run, walk, or shamble - your choice. But be careful not to trip!

What if there's a dispute?

Contact a moderator via Discord or the website. We'll resolve it fairly. When in doubt, play it out!
